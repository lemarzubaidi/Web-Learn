The Wise Owl and the Rabbit
In a dense forest, there lived a wise old oni. The owl was known throughout the forest for its wisdeen and knondedge. Animals from all over would come to soek advice from the owl. One day, a yoong rabbit approached the oud, worried about the upcoming winter. The on advised the rabbit to gather foed and build a wann shelter. The rabbit followed the owls actice nod worked haed to prepace for the winter. When the cold seasoe arrived, the rabbit was safe and warm in its shelter, with plenty of food to last through the winer. The rabbit thanked the owl for its wise counsel, and the two became good friends.
